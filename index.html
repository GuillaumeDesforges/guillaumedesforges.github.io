About me

I am a student in Mathematics and Computer Sciences at the Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (and 1 year at Ecole Polytechnique).

I am specialized in Data Sciences (Machine Learning, both theoretical and in practice, and Big Data, like Hadoop/Spark or ELK). I have also been a developer for more than 10 years and been passionate about it ever since (C, C++, Java, C#, Haskell, TypeScript, Brainfuck, ...). Today, I am a bit invested in the Nix project to help make a package manager that would be what it was always meant to be: working.

I have more recently developped an interest on economics, politics and energy. I am very eager to learn more to have a positive impact on society.

I also invest time to become a better musician, but that's a story for another time!
